About us



Cycle Torbay Community Interest Company (CIC) is a social enterprise offering cycling focussed educational and healthy activities with an aim of promoting cycling as a form of active travel in Torbay. Our mission is to see communities thrive through cycling, and this sits at the core of our activities.

Our Core Aims:

Upskill participants through our Learn-to-Fix Workshops

Build confidence through our Social Bike Rides

Improved health and wellbeing outcomes as participants thrive through cycling and a better environment.
